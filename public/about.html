What is TBOAD?

TBOAD was created to give a voice to the up and coming blue-collar developers of the world.

We aim to provide insight into the lives of students on their journey to become devs and to support the learning community. We want to let immersive and boot camp students know that others understand what they are going through and that they can do it! We also wanted to learn how to make podcasts and have fun. Please contact us with any suggestions, comments, or questions!
